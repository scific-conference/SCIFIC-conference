Conference Materials

Registration

Abstracts of reports and applications for participation must be sent using the Google form (press "Register" button), by November 15, 2026.

Theses are sent electronically with extension «.doc» or «.docx» and name according to template: SURNAME-group-Section_(1,2)-Title_of_work. (Example: YUDIN-555im-Section_1-Protection_In_ICS)

Participation is free. Printed version costs $15 plus shipping. Payment details: scific@csn.khai.edu

Important! Participants who plan to receive printed theses must choose the appropriate option in the form.

FAQ

What is the format of the conference?
The conference is held in an online format.
What are the working languages?
Ukrainian or English.
Can I submit several abstracts from one author?
Yes, one author can submit unlimited abstracts.
Can I publish already printed abstracts?
No, only original works.
Will there be an ISBN?
Yes, the collection will have ISBN.
Is personal presence mandatory?
Participants must attend online meetings in person.
How will online access be organized?
Instructions sent by email to registered participants.
Are participant certificates provided?
Yes, electronic certificates.
Can I speak without submitting an abstract?
No, abstract submission is required.
What is the deadline for submitting papers?
November 15, 2025.
What is the format for submitting papers?
.doc or .docx, name: SURNAME-Group-Section_(1,2)-Title_of_paper.
How to get a printed collection?
Select "I need a printed collection" during registration and pay the fee.
